Volunteer

Mentor teams

We send volunteers out to teams to help them with their robot. You don't need to be an expert already; it's most important to encourage teams to get things done, and to help them solve problems.

"I expected it to be more like teaching, but it was more about supporting the teams. I think that's a good idea because it doesn't waste their time." — Andy Barrett-Sprot

Competition

We run three types of event a year: Kickstart, Tech Days and the Competition itself. Especially at the competition, we're in need of volunteers for many different roles, including setting up, doing safety checks, coordinating teams, scoring games, and many others. No previous experience necessary.

Development

All our software and hardware is open source (see our Git repositories). If you're interested in contributing, pop onto our IRC channel or the srobo-devel mailing list.

Volunteers getting together
