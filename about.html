What is Student Robotics?

Our goal is to inspire the next generation of engineers and software developers.

What we do

Every year we run a competition for students aged 16 to 18. We provide teams a robotics kit and challenge them to go away and build a robot in time for the competition. The best part is, thanks to our sponsors, it's free to enter.

The annual schedule

Kickstart

At the beginning of the academic year we have an event we call a Kickstart. Teams are given their kits and get to do some basic exercises to see how it all works. They're also introduced to the game they'll be playing at the competition.

Tech Days

Throughout the academic year we host a series of tech days in various locations throughout the UK. Usually hosted on a weekend, teams can bring their robots and get some help from volunteers.

The Competition

In the spring we host a competition for all of our participating schools. Teams bring their robots for a weekend of tinkering and matches. Successful teams advance into the knockout stages, and can go on to win prizes.
